Hi Basecamp,

I'm Mark. I'm a Full Stack Ruby Programmer.

Working at Basecamp has been a dream of mine for years now.

In your career, you don't always get the chance to work for a company that makes products that you love to use. As a long time user of Basecamp, and now an active user of HEY, I couldn't be more excited about the idea of contributing in the role of Senior Programmer.

What can I bring to Basecamp?

  • A strong track record of putting Rails to work and bringing products to life.
    In my current role, I work with a legacy Rails application to shape and deliver new features alongside product and design. In the past, I built a Rails application from the ground up, working alongside the CEO and designer to take their ideas and turn them into something intuitive, effective, and user-friendly. From roles as sole programmer to member of a large team, I've discovered how to find a balance between hitting tight deadlines and producing work polished enough to be proud of shipping.
  • Simple solutions to difficult problems.
    Give me ownership and responsibility and I won't let you down. I will brainstorm with co-workers and revise my approach until we find a simple, understandable solution. If a project demands it, I won't hesitate to learn a new skill or research alternative approaches, sometimes a simpler solution is lurking around the corner. When necessary, I'll be pragmatic about a hotfix or deadline.
  • Conscientious, empathetic work.
    I view my work as my craft, something that I constantly improve upon and ultimately stand behind. When it comes down to it, we're writing code and building products for other humans. Empathy is a way to make sure those other humans have the greatest chance at enjoying what you have built. As I dig into problems and search for solutions, I always try to filter my approaches through different perspectives to ensure the greatest chance of success.

Why Basecamp?

  • HEY is pioneering a new path forward.
    For years, I've searched for a way to obtain SPA-like functionality but still stay firmly anchored in the Rails ecosystem. With the launch of HEY, I feel like you have created something really special, and the answer is closer than ever. It is so exciting to think about working with and contributing to that solution.
  • Rails was born here.
    Having spent a few years doing Java web development, Rails carries extra weight for me. It's not just a popular framework - it's something that changed my life. Since having the chance to do professional Rails development, my job satisfaction has gone through the roof. I would love the chance to contribute to new patterns and ideas that other developers may one day benefit from.
  • An amazing team.
    Sure, Basecamp checks all the boxes (and then some) when it comes to perks, but working here is about more than great benefits. Who you work with is often more important than where you work and what you work on. I would be thrilled to work alongside and learn from team members at Basecamp who I admire and who consistently produce thoughtful, quality work.

Let me show you what I can do.
